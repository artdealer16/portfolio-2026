02 / Profile

I am interested in building portfolios and product surfaces that feel both rigorous and unexpected. My process usually starts with a clean typographic structure, then pushes into overlaps, pacing, and small moments of tension that make the composition feel alive.

That balance between structure and disruption is what draws me to Swiss-inspired layouts. I like using disciplined grids as the base, then letting type drift across image fields and negative space so the final result feels more authored than templated.
